NASA Pic of the Day

Download it free for Windows
Receive daily wallpaper updates featuring stunning high resolution images directly from the NASA Image of the Day gallery.
Details about the image and the space and science information behind it are easily accessible through the tray icon.
When the application starts it will automatically update the wallpaper as long as an internet connection is available. You can also manually check for updates. Set the application to run on startup to receive new wallpapers automatically after your computer loads.
Multiple monitors supported.
Find a bug? Submit an issue.

Developed at the 2013 International Space Apps Challenge
