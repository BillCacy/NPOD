Receive daily wallpaper updates featuring stunning high resolution images directly from the NASA Image of the Day gallery.

The daily image is saved to ~/Pictures/nasaiotd.jpg on Mac, and C:\Users\your_username\Pictures\NASA\PicOfTheDay on Windows.
Details about the image and the space and science information behind it are easily accessible through the tray icon.

The application will automatically update the wallpaper when it starts and daily at 10:30a EST for as long as it is running and an internet connection is available. You can also manually check for updates.
Multiple monitors supported.
